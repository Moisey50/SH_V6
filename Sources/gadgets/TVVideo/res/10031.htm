Location in gadget tree: Filters \Video\color&brightness for grouped by type or Video\color&brightness\ by default

VideoFlatten2 is similar to VideoFlatten Gadget, but makes contrast equalizing more aggressively, and not only in shadows, but in regions of flare. The sample of the processing is shown below:

Gadget setup dialog has one parameter - ConvParameter - it can be from 0 to 10, and determine the radius of the effect in arbitrary units. Then less parameter, then less zone will be normalized.
