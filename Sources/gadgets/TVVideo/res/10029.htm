VideoFeatureDetector gadget
Input Formats:CVideoFrame
Outputs Formats:CVideoFrame
Duplex Formats: NONE
Location in gadget tree: Filters \Video\featuredetectors\ for grouped by type or Video\featuredetectors\ by default

VideoFeatureDetector gadget aggressively seeks zones of high density of the contrast elements. The output frame has twice decreased sizes. The algorithm based on sum of absolute differences of points around target point.

 

Gadget has no setup dialog.
