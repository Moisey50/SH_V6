VideoClearFrames gadget
Input Formats:CVideoFrame
Outputs Formats:CVideoFrame
Duplex Formats: NONE
Location in gadget tree: Filters \Video\frames for grouped by type or Video\frame\ by default

VideoClearFrames Filter clear border of frame with predefined color.

Gadget setup dialog:

Fil_Color - the color of the frame.

Margin - the thickness of the frame.
