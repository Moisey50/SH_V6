Locator gadget
 
   Input Formats:     VFrame

Outputs Formats:  VFrame

Duplex Formats:   NONE

Locator gadget converts input video frame to video frame in polar coordinates relative to predefined point. The output image has angle coordinates (0-1000) on X, and radius coordinate as Y. The sample of transformation can be seen below:

->

  The setup parameters:

 

Central point location for polar transformation can be pointed by clicking in the image or by digits in corresponding field of setup dialog.

Locator params:

Angle: can be 180° or 360° - the angle range for transform. The start angle correspond 9 hours on a clock.

Radius: the maximal radius for transform, if -1 the radius is height of the image.
